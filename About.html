My name is Molly Candon and I am an artist living in Ballysadare, Co. Sligo. I completed my BA in Visual Art Practice in IADT in Dun Laoghaire, where I participated in an exchange year in Sabanci University, Istanbul. I have exhibited my work in Dublin, Sligo, Istanbul, and Wroklaw (Poland).
